Generative Adversarial Networks

Fake structural framing and core

Description

This is the use of a generative adversarial network (GAN) to generate fake images of structural floor framing plans. A GAN is similar to a counterfeiter which mimics what it has seen in training. In this example, the GAN is trying to build a building core and structural framing.

I opened 50+ Revit models to capture an image of structural floor framing from each model. This created 650 images for the data set. I ran it through Nvidia's Style GAN for 5 days to generate fake structural framing plans.
